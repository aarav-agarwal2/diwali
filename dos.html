below are the do's and dont's for an eco-friendly diwali.


DO'S

  1. Distribute sweets amongst your neighbours, friends, and family.
  2. distribute food, clothes, sweets, and some money to poor people.
  3. Spend time with your family playing games, or talking to each other, instead of staying online.
  4. Video call your friends or relatives who live very far away.
  5. You can also donate blood. This will be more helpful, mostly during this pandemic.

DONT'S

  1. Do not, and do not burst crackers. These have harmful toxins that will make you
    ill, directly or indirectly.
  2. Do not go to any grand party for diwali, as there is a lot of food wasted.
  3. Do not cut trees indirectly. Instead plant a tree.
  4. Do not launch flying lanterns in the sky, as it can disrupt an airplane, or hit a bird.
